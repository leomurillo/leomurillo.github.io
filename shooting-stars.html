✨ Why Continuous Ratings?

Traditional 5-star systems force artificial decisions between discrete values, losing valuable nuance in user feedback.

  • Natural Expression: Rate exactly how you feel—3.7 stars, not forced to choose between 3 or 4
  • Cognitive Alignment: We consume ratings with decimal precision (4.3★) but are forced to give integer feedback—this creates dissonance
  • Information Preservation: Every bit of user sentiment matters. Why throw away precision when users can provide it effortlessly?

"The best interface is one that matches how users think, not how systems compute."
