Parametric Mapping of 3-vector trangulation to the circle

We show two sin functions, at 2x speed, one scaled by a magic number and one offset by Pi/3. You can add another Pi/3 in the angle of the sin functions and get the same. So you get three of these, before looping.

See image source here. See the ortogonal or cartesian parametric mapping adding another Pi/3 here.
